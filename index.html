Tracking Trash and Debris Cleanups in Price George's County Watersheds

Form and User Inputs

Both the text input filter and dropdown menu filter perform the same functionality. They will reduce the dataset to only cleanups that used a certain number of bags to finish. For example, you may be interested in small cleanups, and so filtering to show cleanups that only needed 1 bag would be a good place to start. Alternatively, you may be interested in large cleanups, so filter to only show cleanups that needed 10 bags to complete.

**Note: For the text input filter, only whole numbers from 1-10 inclusive are valid.
